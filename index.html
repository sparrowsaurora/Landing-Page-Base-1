Is there a free trial available?

Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for new users. You can explore all features without any commitment. No credit card is required to sign up.

Can I change my plan later?

Absolutely! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time through your account settings. Any changes will take effect at the end of your current billing cycle.

What is the refund policy?

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee. Please contact our support team within 30 days of your purchase for a full refund.

How do I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time by going to your account settings and selecting the cancellation option. Your subscription will remain active until the end of the current billing period.

Can I change my account's email?

Yes, you can change your account email address by accessing the account settings. Just enter your new email address and follow the verification steps.

How does billing work?

Billing is processed on a monthly or annual basis, depending on the plan you choose. You will receive an invoice via email for each billing cycle.
